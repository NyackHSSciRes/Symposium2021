Project Summary

Over the last few decades climate change has been taking a huge toll on our wildlife and ecosystems. The greenhouse gas effect is the increasing surface temperature of the earth's crust due to an excessive release of greenhouse gases like methane or carbon dioxide. These awful conditions force animal species to migrate or even die off until extinction within the next few decades. My topic will focus on one of these species in the Hudson Valley region and I will test exactly how much climate change affects our local wildlife. I will need to use many databases and websites in order to collect my research as well as doing some of my own.
